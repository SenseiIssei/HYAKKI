## The Ten Kings 十王

Ten documented judges of the dead, each pronouncing at a set interval after death. They
replace the ten Wardens exactly, and they are a *better* fit: a boss every tenth Ri that
**judges** you rather than merely blocks you.

**They are not killed.** You are dismissed, or you are not. Copy never says "defeated" —
it says the hearing ended. This keeps a living religious figure out of the position of loot.

| # | King | Judges on | Signature | What it does | First Ri |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **SHINKŌ-Ō** 秦広王 | 7th day | **THE FIRST HEARING** | Summons attendants — the ISSUE mechanic, now clerks | 10 |
| 2 | **SHOKŌ-Ō** 初江王 | 14th day | **THE CROSSING** | Meets you at Sanzu; floods the ground, halving your speed | 20 |
| 3 | **SŌTEI-Ō** 宋帝王 | 21st day | **THE ACCOUNT** | Reads what you did; each item read is +8% to its attack | 40 |
| 4 | **GOKAN-Ō** 五官王 | 28th day | **THE SCALES** | Weighs you: whichever of your stats is highest is set to your lowest for 6s | 70 |
| 5 | **ENMA-Ō** 閻魔王 | 35th day | **JŌHARI NO KAGAMI** 浄玻璃の鏡 | The mirror that shows every deed. Copies your entire statline at 90%. | 110 |
| 6 | **HENSEI-Ō** 変成王 | 42nd day | **THE ALTERATION** | Changes what you are — your class Pipeline is disabled for 8s | 160 |
| 7 | **TAIZAN-Ō** 泰山王 | 49th day | **THE ALLOTMENT** | Decides your next life; heals to 60% once | 220 |
| 8 | **BYŌDŌ-Ō** 平等王 | 100th day | **EQUALITY** | Its health is set to the number you have felled this walk | 300 |
| 9 | **TOSHI-Ō** 都市王 | 1 year | **THE REGISTER** | Silences your In for the hearing | 400 |
| 10 | **GODŌ-TENRIN-Ō** 五道転輪王 | 3 years | **THE WHEEL** | Erasure. Your Armor and Evasion read zero. | 500 |

Enma is assisted by two disembodied heads — **Miru-me** 見る目, who perceives secret faults,
and **Kagu-hana** 嗅ぐ鼻, who smells out misdeeds. They are his two guards in the ISSUE
mechanic and they are *named on screen*, which is much worse than being anonymous.

**Tier 2 (after Enshrinement)** appends 「，改」 — *amended*. Tier 3 appends 「，最終」 — *final*.

## The classes

Pipelines and curses are unchanged from MYRIAD — the numbers are tuned. Only identity moves.

### ⬡ NIŌ 仁王 — *the gate that is a person*
> *There are two of them at every gate. There has only ever been one of you.*

Damage scales off Armor. `BRACE` becomes **KONGŌ** 金剛 — *adamantine*: take nothing for
three seconds, then return all of it at once. Curse: −25% speed. Grant: 20 Armor.

### ⬡ KITSUNEBI 狐火 — *it was not lit and it does not go out*
> *Follow it and you will arrive somewhere. It will not be where you were going.*

Weak hits that stack Burn. `FLASHPOINT` becomes **HITODAMA** 人魂 — detonate every stack.
Wants attack speed above everything.

### ⬡ ONMYŌJI 陰陽師 — *has read the almanac and is not reassured*
> *It knows which days are unlucky. Every day here is one of them.*

Crit and foresight. `FORESIGHT` becomes **SHIKIGAMI** 式神 — six certain strikes at double
multiplier. Curse: −50% health.

### ⬡ ONRYŌ 怨霊 — *earned by dying twenty-five times*
> *It is not angry with you. It is angry, and you are here.*

Damage scales with your death count this Ascension. `SECOND BODY` becomes **URAMI** 恨み —
keep going six seconds past the killing blow. **Cannot heal. The dead do not mend.**

### ⬡ KUCHIYOSE 口寄せ — *earned at ten stackings*
> *Ask how many it is. The answer changes while it speaks.*

Summons **kage** — your own past walks — as Echoes. `THE CHOIR` becomes **YOBIDASHI** 呼び出し.
Curse: −30% speed; kage die permanently for the walk.

### ⬡ ONBŌ 隠亡 — *earned at five thousand felled*
> *It follows the Parade at a distance. It is the only one here with a job that ends.*

×2.5 Tama, ×1.8 Ishi, +50% time away. Genuinely bad at fighting. The overnight class.

### ⬡ MUMYŌ 無名 — *8 Na*
> *No name. Not lost — never given.*

The Cairn does nothing. Only what you carry counts.

### ⬡ JŪŌ 十王 — *12 Na*
> *You have stood in front of this. You are wearing it.*

You take a King's statline and its clock — at **every** Ri.

### ⬡ YAMABUSHI 山伏 — *3 Na, Meguri only*
Knows the paths down. Reveals rooms, +100% tsukumogami, poor in the Parade.

unequipped for the duration. You get it back. You always get it back.

## Hyakumonogatari 百物語 — the archive

One hundred candles, one hundred stories, one candle snuffed after each.

- Each Kaidan read **snuffs a candle**. The Archive's background darkens by 1% per story.
- At **99** the screen is nearly black and the last card is unlit.
- At **100** — the room is dark, and the thing the game has said would arrive, arrives. This
  is the Hyakki Yagyō encounter (the MYRIAD fight, re-themed): the whole Parade, made of
  every kage you have recorded.

**Currently written: 30 of 100.** The counter shows the true number. Padding to 100 with
weak stories would be worse than being 30 deep and honest — the remaining 70 are a content
task, not a system task.

## New systems the folklore demands

### KEGARE 穢れ — defilement
A slow accumulating impurity: +1 per Judgment failed, +1 per hell room entered, +1 per
hundred felled. At thresholds it does things — enemies notice you sooner, Tama pays less,
and past 100 the Mu begin appearing regardless of depth.

**Purified by** stacking stones (the reset), or by an **ofuda**.

### OFUDA 御札 — wards
Consumable paper talismans, dropped and bought. Single-use, chosen before a walk, up to 3.
*Nullify the next Judgment's Signature. Halve Kegare gain for the walk. Survive one killing
blow. Make one Mu targetable.*

This is the one genuinely new mechanic, and it exists because **wards are how you actually
survive yōkai** — a game about them without paper charms is missing the point.
